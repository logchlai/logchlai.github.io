Foundation Industrial (China) Limited

is a leading Hong Kong manufacturer and exporter of security products. Founded in 1972, our company occupied a facility of 400 square feet with five employees. Today our turnover exceeds US$ 6 million, with facilities in a number of locations.

Our own properties in Hong Kong comprise over 10,000 ft2 and are used as the head quarter of the company, R & D, QC, and warehouse. A further 5,000 ft2 are used for the plastic injection department and the tooling workshop .

In Dongguan, Guangdong Province of China we employ more than 150 staff with a premises of 20,000 ft2 for the production. We specialize in the fields of plastics injection, electronics, sheet metal forming, die-casting metal forming, & etc. We are also doing a lot of OEM businesses with our overseas customers in the fields of plastics, electronics, sheet metals, metal die-castings & tooling. We have our own tooling workshops both in Hong Kong and China equipped with 2 CNC machines. In Canada, we have a joint venture company, Leister Blake Enterprises Ltd., located in Vancouver. This company is specializing in plastic tooling & parts for industrial purposes as well as the excellent design ability on RF products & tooling.
